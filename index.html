We're Muddest and we love obstacle races!

The comraderie, the physical and mental challenge, and the absolute satisfaction of having done what is hard and overcoming your obstacles – this is why we do it.

It doesn't matter if you're a hardcore, trifecta veteran, or someone who haven't run an obstacle race yet. As long as you're not afraid to get muddy, break a sweat, and maybe shed a tear, then you'll fit right in on Muddest!
