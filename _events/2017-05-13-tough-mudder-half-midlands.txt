Awesome Venue, New Course
In 2017 we are taking you back to the EPIC grounds of Belvoir Castle for Tough Mudder Half Midlands Presented by Merrell. The majestic 16,000 acres surrounding the castle will once again play host to mud, obstacles and good times.

5 Miles, 13 Obstacles
You and your team are going to need to be prepared to go all out and tackle a tricky combination of hills, woodlands, water and, yes, tons and tons of mud. It's best you don't contemplate it for too long, just sign up and gather your friends to join in the fun.
